Welcome to Suburbia Park

Welcome to Suburbia Park, an established residential neighborhood in south east Huntington Beach consisting of 142 one and two story single family homes. Built in 1967, the homes are bounded on the east by the Santa Ana River, on the west by Brookhurst Street, on the north by Meredith Gardens and on the south by the Shorecrest East.
