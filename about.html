Welcome to Suburbia Park, an established residential neighborhood in south east Huntington Beach consisting of 142 one and two story single family homes. Built in 1967, the homes are bounded on the east by the Santa Ana River, on the west by Brookhurst Street, on the north by Meredith Gardens and on the south by the Shorecrest East.

At the northeast corner of the development, Lebard Park provides open recreation space with two public tennis courts, a play area and six baseball diamonds, home to the Seaview Little League. Suburbia Park is located just two miles from Huntington State Beach, which is easily accessed via the Santa Ana River bike trail. Just a few miles north on PCH, Huntington City beach, Pacific City, the famous Huntington Beach pier and the friendly downtown area provide dining and entertainment for all ages.

Suburbia Park is part of the Huntington Beach school district which includes many California Distinguished Award schools on all levels, as well as a number of excellent local private schools.

The development is conveniently located a few miles from the 405 freeway providing easy access to the rest of Orange County and other Southern California cities and places of interest.

Suburbia Park is a great place to raise a family, retire or just to live in peace and quiet. Welcome to our neighborhood!
