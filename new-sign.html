New Sign

New Suburbia Park Sign

As you may have noticed, someone vandalized the sign to the entrance of our tract. It was partially torn off the wall, badly bent and now partially removed.

  • A small committee of neighbors has volunteered to help get a new “updated” sign put back up to replace the damaged one. If you are interested in helping us, please contact one of your neighbors listed below.
  • We are also looking for donations to help pay for the new sign which is estimated to cost somewhere from $1000 to $3,000. The more we collect, the nicer the sign. Any amount will help, but remember, the sign represents each home in our community.
  • You may visit our website www.suburbiapark.org to track how much money we have collected as well as the progress of the project.

Sometime in the next few weeks, one of your neighbors will stop by your house with a rough draft of the proposed artwork for the sign. At that time we hope you would be willing to make a donation towards the cost of the sign and participate however you can.

This new neighborhood tract sign will be a beautiful representation of the pride and character we have for our neighborhood.
